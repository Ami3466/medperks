# Privacy Policy — Care Companion

_Last updated: 26 June 2026_

Care Companion ("the app") is a medication-adherence tool for families. This policy
explains what the app does with your information.

## The short version
- The app is **local-first**: your data (medications, schedule, prizes, dose history) is
  stored **on your device**, not on our servers.
- The **camera and microphone** are used only to record a short clip when a dose is taken,
  so the dose can be confirmed.
- We do **not** sell your data, show ads, or use third-party analytics or tracking.

## What we collect and why
- **Medication, schedule, prizes, and dose records** — what you enter to run the app.
  Stored locally on your device. Not transmitted to us.
- **Camera & microphone** — to record a short dose-confirmation clip. When AI
  confirmation is enabled, individual still frames may be sent to our AI provider
  (Google) only to return a confirmation result; they are not used to train models and
  are not retained beyond producing that result.
- **Notifications** — local reminders scheduled on your device at the dose times you set.

## What we do NOT do
- No advertising and no ad SDKs.
- No third-party analytics or tracking SDKs.
- No sale or sharing of your personal data.

## Health information
The app stores medication-adherence information you enter. It stays on your device and
under your control. The app is a support tool and does **not** provide medical advice.

## Data retention & deletion
Because data is stored on your device, uninstalling the app (or "Switch role" / reset)
removes it. Recorded dose clips, when stored, are kept only 30–90 days, then deleted.

## Children
Care Companion is intended for adult caregivers and the people they care for. It is not
directed at children under 13.

## Changes
We may update this policy; the "last updated" date above will change.
